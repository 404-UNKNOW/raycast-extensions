# Ramen Changelog

## [1.0.0] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

### Added
- Initial release of Ramen Dashboard.
- Real-time revenue tracking for Stripe and Lemon Squeezy.
- Visual "Ramen Bowl" progress indicator in the menu bar.
- Dynamic Sparklines for 7-day revenue trends.
- Privacy Mode to hide revenue amounts with a shortcut (`Cmd+Shift+H`).
- Quick Actions to share milestones on X (Twitter) (`Cmd+Shift+S`).
- Recent transactions list with direct links to dashboard.
- Support for custom monthly goals and currency symbols.
