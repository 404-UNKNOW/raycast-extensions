# 🍜 Ramen: Track MRR from your Mac Menu Bar

We all share the same addiction: **refreshing the Stripe dashboard 30 times a day.** 

Waiting for the next sale. Staring at the blank charts. It kills focus, causes anxiety, and ruins deep work.

I built **Ramen** to cure this. 

Ramen sits quietly in your macOS menu bar. It translates your revenue into visual milestones.

*Is the bowl empty? Time to ship.* 
*Is the bowl full? Congratulations, you are Ramen Profitable.*

---

### ⚡️ Why Ramen?

- **👀 Glanceable Motivation**: Your revenue is a glance away. From a cold empty bowl (0 sales) to a hot steaming Ramen bowl (MRR goal reached). 
- **🔒 100% Privacy & Local-First**: Your API keys *never* leave your Mac. Ramen fetches data securely via Raycast's native environment straight to your APIs. No middleware, no analytics, no bullshit.
- **⚡️ Gamified Progress**: Set your "Ramen Goal" (monthly basic living expenses) and watch the progress bar fill up in real-time. 

### ⚙️ Features
- **Direct Stripe Support**: Plug in your read-only restricted key.
- **Stealth Mode**: Hide the numbers with one shortcut when screen-sharing.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Dropdown reveals your latest 5 sales so you never miss a ping.

## Setup & Development

1. **Install dependencies**: `npm install`
2. **Run in development**: `npm run dev`
3. **Configure**: Enter your Stripe Restricted Key (Read-only for Balance and Transactions).

## Security & Compliance
- **Keychain**: API keys are stored in macOS Encrypted Storage.
- **Direct Connect**: Peer-to-peer connection between your Mac and Stripe.
